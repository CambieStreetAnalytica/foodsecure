AMS FoodBank

Details

On each visit, individual customers receive one bag of groceries, and families receive two bags. New customers receive a complimentary reusable bag on their first visit. Make sure you keep it and bring it back next time you visit to avoid paying $2 for another one! We operate on a trust-based system and will not turn any UBC student away.

Usage

All current UBC students requiring emergency food relief can use the Food Bank up to six times a term. This policy is in place to ensure that supplies are only used in emergency situations and not for supplementary purposes. Terms run from September – December, January – April and May – August.

Hours

We are currently open on Mondays and Wednesdays from 10AM – 4PM.

More Info

If our resources are not meeting your needs, our staff will provide referrals to other Lower Mainland food banks and sources of financial assistance.
